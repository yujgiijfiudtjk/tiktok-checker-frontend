How RX Downloader works
№ Guide

Three steps. No login. No watermark. No data kept on our servers.

1
Copy the TikTok video link
Open the TikTok app or website, tap Share on any video, then choose Copy link. You can use the short vt.tiktok.com form or the full URL.
2
Paste it above & press Download
RX Downloader resolves the video through a clean, watermark-stripping backend. Nothing is logged — the request is stateless and ephemeral.
3
Pick your format
You get three direct download buttons: MP4 (No Watermark), MP4 (HD) and MP3 (Audio only). Tap any to save locally.
4
Preview & share
You can preview the video inline before saving, copy the report, or share RX Downloader using the buttons at the bottom.
