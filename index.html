How AH Analytics works
№ 00 · Guide

AH Analytics is a precision instrument for TikTok profile forensics. In four short steps, you get verified metadata, account age, engagement signals and commerce data — all in real-time, with zero retention.

i
Enter a TikTok username
Type any public handle in the search field above (without the @). Only letters, numbers, dots and underscores are allowed — minimum 2 characters.
ii
Press Inspect
AH Analytics fetches the profile through an encrypted, rate-limited backend. Nothing is logged or stored — the request is one-way and stateless.
iii
Read the full report
You get the profile's creation date, total age in days, followers/likes/views, region & language, commerce status, shop info, and content interaction settings (Duet, Stitch, Download, Comment).
iv
Export or share
Use the Copy button at the bottom to export a plain-text report (with optional extra fields), or share AH Analytics with others using the social buttons below.
